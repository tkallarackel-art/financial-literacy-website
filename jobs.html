What happens if I don't pay my taxes

  1. You will recieve letters and emails remining you that you have tax to pay.
  2. Then they start adding interest to the tax you owe
  3. Then you can start getting penalty fees ontop of the interest and tax
  4. Then the detp collection process will start. The IRD can take money from your account, take money from your wages before you even get paid, impound your can and even stop you from getting any refund in the future.
  5. In extreme cases you could even get very large fines, court appearances and even prison time
